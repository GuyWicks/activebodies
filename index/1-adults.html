ADULT BUJINKAN

Norman Smithers

Bujinkan Kouryoo Dojo Huddersfield

Norman has been involved with martial arts for over thirty years and with the Bujinkan since 1982. Previously Norman served five years in the French Foreign Legion and nine years in the British Army.

Norman has been awarded the rank Jugodan (15th dan) by Hatsumi sensei

Guy Wicks

Bujinkan Instructor

Guy has been training in the Bujinkan for ten years and has a several years of experience teaching adults, teenagers, and kids classes.

In 2016, Guy was awarded his Sandan (3rd dan) and travelled to Tokyo Japan where he trained with Someya sensei and Nagato sensei.

Adam Davidson

Bujinkan Instructor

Adam has a background in judo, jujutsu and has been training in the Bujinkan for over ten years.

Adam is a Sandan (3rd dan) and frequently teaches adults at Norman's dojo on a Sunday.
